The large Proportion of Variance for PC1 explains that 99.8% of the variance is accounted for by component 1. Thus, if we drop the rest of the components, we still have 99.8% of the variability of Wine dataset.

b. Comment on the use of normalization (standardization) in part (a).

Performing normalization depends on the nature of the data. In the Wine dataset, there is essentially no need to normalize the PCA results since the measurement is the same for all variables.
